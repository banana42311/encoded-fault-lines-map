Encoded Fault Lines

A crowdsourced map of network accessibility in London.

About this map

Each building is coloured by the average latency measured inside it. Pulsing points represent individual measurements, where the animation encodes local instability and packet loss.

How to contribute

  1. Pan and zoom the map to your location.
  2. Click on the map to add a measurement.
  3. Enter the latency, jitter, and packet loss you observed.

As more values are added inside a building, its colour shifts to reflect the collective measurements.
